About the archives

YoRHa Archives is a lightweight static journal inspired by the terminal readouts used by the android forces in the bunker. Entries are rendered directly in the browser—no database, no build step.

Update assets/js/posts.js and refresh the page to decrypt new field reports.

Write your own log

Add a new object to window.BLOG_POSTS and include these fields:

  1. title – the operation name or headline.
  2. date – timestamp in YYYY-MM-DD format.
  3. excerpt – a short teaser for the mission.
  4. body – the full briefing. Separate paragraphs with a blank line; use **bold** and `code` for emphasis.
